B.Tech. Computer Science & Engineering

Programme Specific Outcomes

Graduates of the B.Tech. CSE programme at Marwadi University will demonstrate the following domain-specific competencies on successful completion of their degree.

01

Software Design & Development

Ability to analyse, design, develop, and evaluate software systems using contemporary programming paradigms, software engineering practices, and industry-standard tools — from requirements gathering through deployment and maintenance.

02

Computing Systems & Networks

Competence in understanding and designing computer architecture, operating systems, and communication networks — including system calls, process management, memory hierarchy, routing protocols, and network security fundamentals.

03

Data, AI & Intelligent Systems

Proficiency in applying algorithms, data structures, database systems, statistical methods, and machine learning models to extract actionable insights and build intelligent, data-driven applications for real-world problem-solving.

04

Cybersecurity & Cloud Computing

Understanding of information security principles, cryptographic techniques, cloud service models, and infrastructure design — enabling students to architect secure, scalable, and resilient systems aligned with modern enterprise needs.

05

Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Ethics

Capability to identify technological opportunities, apply design thinking, and build innovative products — while demonstrating professional integrity, teamwork, effective communication, and ethical responsibility in engineering practice.
