Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

The guiding principles that shape every decision, curriculum, and experience at Marwadi University.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning, fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and excellence — producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to society, industry, and the nation through knowledge, character, and competence.

  • Global recognition in research and education
  • Holistic development of every student
  • Creating future-ready innovators & leaders

Our Mission

To deliver outcome-based, industry-aligned education that empowers students with technical expertise, analytical thinking, and ethical values — through state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, international collaborations, and a vibrant research ecosystem.

  • Outcome-based education model (OBE)
  • Industry partnerships & real-world exposure
  • Fostering ethical values and social responsibility
Academic Outcomes

Program Specific
Outcomes (PSOs)

Defined competencies that graduates of the B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering (Cybersecurity) program will demonstrate upon completion.

01
Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Apply core principles of information security, cryptography, and network defense to design and evaluate secure computing systems.

02
Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing

Perform authorized vulnerability assessments and penetration tests using industry-standard tools (Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite).

03
Network Security & Forensics

Analyze network traffic, investigate security incidents, and apply digital forensics techniques to identify and respond to cyber threats.

04
Secure Software Development

Develop and audit software following secure coding standards, mitigating vulnerabilities such as OWASP Top-10 in web and mobile applications.

05
Compliance & Risk Management

Understand and apply cybersecurity frameworks (ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR) to manage organizational security risk and regulatory compliance.

06
Research & Innovation

Contribute to the cybersecurity research community through publications, CTF competitions, and development of novel security tools and algorithms.
